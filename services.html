Visit FAQ

Answer the questions people usually keep to themselves.

A strong church site anticipates practical questions and answers them plainly instead of forcing someone to ask by email.

What should I wear? +
Come as you are. The aim is not to impress anyone with a dress code but to gather with openness before God.
Should I come early if it is my first time? +
Yes. Arriving 10 to 15 minutes early gives you space to settle in, meet someone from the team, and avoid rushing into the room.
Can I start with Wednesday instead of Sunday? +
Yes. Hope Nights are a good way to enter the rhythm of the church if midweek is the easiest first step for you.
What if I need help with directions before I come? +
Email hello@onebodychurch.org before you set out. The team can help you confirm the route and answer any final questions.
